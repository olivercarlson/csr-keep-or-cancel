Keep Or Cancel for The Sapphire Reserve v2.0

This guide is specifically aimed at helping you make a decision about whether or not you would like to keep or cancel the Sapphire Reserve credit card.

This will walk you through a sequential series of questions and return a verdict unique to your situation about whether or not you should continue keeping the card and paying the $450 annual fee.

Hovering over the information icon will give you some guidance for each question.
Question 1: How much do you value each Ultimate Rewards Point?
Enter a value between 1.00 and 5.00 cents per point


Question 2: How much do you anticipate spending on Travel on the Sapphire Reserve?
Enter an amount between $0 and $10,000,000


Question 3: How much do you anticipate spending on Dining on the Sapphire Reserve?
Enter an amount between $0 and $10,000,000


Question 4: Will you also be using the Freedom Unlimited?


Question 5: How much do you anticipate spending in all other categories on the Sapphire Reserve / Freedom Unlimited?
Enter an amount between $0 and $10,000,000


Question 6: What do you value the $300 Travel Credit at?
Enter an amount up to $300


Question 7: What do you value the Priority Pass Memberhsip at?
Enter a value up to $1,000


Question 8: What do you value the Trip Delay and other insurance at?
Enter a value up to $1,000.
